About Me

Hello, my name is Katelin Frederick and I am a 24 year old self-taught front-end developer currently located in Adrian, Michigan. I am currently hoping to find an opportunity to begin my career in the web industry and am willing to relocate to do so. Currently I work at an automotive plant for a company named Martinrea as an extrusion operator. When I'm not at work or focusing on expanding my development skills, I enjoy playing guitar and listening to music. I love to learn new things and expand upon skills I already have. I have an associates degree in Fine Arts from Jackson College. I intended to continue my education after I recieved that degree, so I went back to pursue a degree in Computer Programming. Unfortunately, I wasn't able to complete that degree for financial reasons, however, the courses I was able to take during this time made me develop a love for programming and development. After I left school, I decided to pursue a career in the industry and began to continue learning on my own.
